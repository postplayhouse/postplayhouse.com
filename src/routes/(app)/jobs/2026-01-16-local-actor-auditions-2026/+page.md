## Casting

Post Playhouse is looking for local kids, teens, and adults to perform alongside professionals in the productions of **_Seussical the Musical_**, **_Oklahoma_**, and **_Grease_**.

Rehearsals for local kids/teens will be in the evenings and weekends beginning May 4, 2026. Once school is out, daytime rehearsals will be added.

## What to Prepare

Please prepare an uptempo song that shows off your personality. Please have a recorded accompaniment with you that we will play through our speakers. You may also sing acapella.

Please bring comfortable clothes and shoes to dance in for a short dance combination.
